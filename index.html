ECHO LOOP

Break into a collapsing neon vault by weaponizing your own past timelines.

What kind of game is this?

It is a clone-puzzle strategy game. Every time you create a loop, your last movement path becomes a glowing echo. Those clones can hold switches, sync pressure pads, and help you open the gate.

How to play

  • Move: W A S D or Arrow Keys
  • Create Loop: Space or Enter
  • Reset Room: R
  • Hint: H
Mission Brief

You are infiltrating the Omega Vault — a living security system that rewinds reality. To beat it, you must use your past selves against it. The final chamber is the Boss Level: a timed lockdown full of hazards, triple-switch sync, and one last gate between you and freedom.
